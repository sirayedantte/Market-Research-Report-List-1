<p><p>The oil package boiler market features three primary types: D-type, A-type, and O-type package boilers. D-type boilers have a cylindrical design with a vertical furnace and are efficient for large outputs. A-type boilers, designed with a dual-drum configuration, offer flexibility and are ideal for varied applications. O-type boilers feature a horizontal layout, providing compactness and ease of installation, commonly used in smaller industrial setups. These boilers cater to diverse industrial needs, emphasizing efficiency and space optimization.</p></p>
