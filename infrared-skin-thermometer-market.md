<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Non-contact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared skin thermometer market is divided into two main types: contact and non-contact thermometers. Contact thermometers require physical touch with the skin to measure temperature accurately, often providing precise readings. In contrast, non-contact thermometers measure temperature from a distance, using infrared technology to detect heat emitted by the skin. Non-contact devices are favored for their convenience and speed, making them suitable for quick screenings, especially in environments like hospitals or during health emergencies.</p></p>
