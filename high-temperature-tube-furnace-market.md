<p><ul><li>Horizontal</li><li>Vertical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High temperature tube furnaces are categorized into horizontal and vertical types based on their design and orientation. Horizontal tube furnaces allow loading and unloading of materials along the horizontal axis, making them suitable for batch processing and larger samples. In contrast, vertical tube furnaces have a vertical configuration, optimizing space usage and providing efficient thermal uniformity, generally suited for smaller samples or continuous processes. Both designs cater to specific applications in research, materials processing, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
