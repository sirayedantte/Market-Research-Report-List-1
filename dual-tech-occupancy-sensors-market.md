<p><p>Dual tech occupancy sensors utilize both passive infrared (PIR) and ultrasonic technologies to detect motion and enhance energy efficiency in lighting control. Ceiling mount occupancy sensors are typically installed on ceilings, providing a wide field of view for detecting occupants in larger areas like offices or auditoriums. Wall mount occupancy sensors, on the other hand, are placed on walls and are effective in smaller spaces or corridors. Both types improve energy savings by ensuring lights are only activated when needed.</p></p>
