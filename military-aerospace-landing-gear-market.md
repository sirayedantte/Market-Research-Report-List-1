<p><strong>The Military Aerospace Landing Gear Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Main Landing Gear</li><li>Nose/Tail Landing Gear</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The military aerospace landing gear market includes two main types: main landing gear and nose/tail landing gear. Main landing gear supports the bulk of the aircraft's weight during landing and takeoff, typically positioned under the fuselage. Nose landing gear, located at the front, provides steering control and stabilizes the aircraft on the ground. Tail landing gear, found on specific aircraft designs, supports the rear and assists during landing maneuvers, ensuring balance and structural integrity for military operations.</p></p>
