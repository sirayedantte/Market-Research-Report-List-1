<p><strong>The Cautery Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unipolar Device</li><li>Bipolar Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cautery machine market is segmented into unipolar and bipolar devices, each serving distinct surgical needs. Unipolar devices use a single electrode with a return pad placed on the patient, allowing for deeper tissue penetration and effective cutting and coagulation. In contrast, bipolar devices utilize two electrodes in the surgical instrument itself, which offers more precision and minimal thermal spread, making it ideal for delicate procedures. Both types enhance surgical efficiency and safety, catering to varying procedural requirements.</p></p>
