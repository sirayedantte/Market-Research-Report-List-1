<p><p>The GaAs laser market comprises three main types: Avalanche Diodes (APDs), Vertical-Cavity Surface-Emitting Lasers (VCSELs), and Edge-Emitting Lasers (EELs). APDs are known for their high sensitivity and capability for signal amplification, making them ideal for long-range communication. VCSELs offer low power consumption and are used in applications like data centers and consumer electronics due to their compact size and efficient operation. EELs provide high output power and are extensively utilized in telecom and industrial applications for their robustness and performance.</p></p>
