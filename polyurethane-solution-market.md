<p><strong>The Polyurethane Solution Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-based Polyurethane Compounds</li><li>Solvent-based Polyurethane Compounds</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane solution market is primarily divided into two types: water-based and solvent-based polyurethane compounds. Water-based polyurethane compounds utilize water as a solvent, making them environmentally friendly and low in volatile organic compounds (VOCs). They are widely used in coatings, adhesives, and sealants. In contrast, solvent-based polyurethane compounds use organic solvents, providing superior durability and chemical resistance. They are favored in applications requiring robust performance, such as wood finishes and industrial coatings, though they may have higher VOC emissions.</p></p>
