<p><p>The flexible display technology market comprises three primary types: Liquid Crystal Display (LCD), Organic Light Emitting Diode (OLED), and Electronic Paper Display (EPD). LCDs utilize liquid crystals sandwiched between layers to create images, suitable for traditional applications. OLEDs, on the other hand, use organic compounds that emit light when electrified, offering vibrant colors and flexibility for modern devices. EPDs mimic printed paper, providing excellent visibility and low power consumption, ideal for e-readers and signage. Each type serves distinct applications and consumer needs.</p></p>
