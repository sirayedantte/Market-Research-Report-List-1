<p><ul><li>Subtractive Dichroic Filters</li><li>Additive Dichroic Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dichroic color filter market is categorized into two main types: subtractive and additive dichroic filters. Subtractive dichroic filters work by absorbing certain wavelengths of light and transmitting others, typically used in applications like photography and stage lighting to modify colors. In contrast, additive dichroic filters combine different colors of light to produce a new hue, often utilized in projection systems and display technologies. Both filter types play crucial roles in enhancing visual experiences across various industries.</p></p>
