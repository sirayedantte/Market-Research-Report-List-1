<p><p>The underground stormwater detention system market is primarily categorized into two types: modular tank systems and chamber systems. Modular tank systems consist of interlocking tanks designed to store and manage stormwater efficiently, allowing for easy installation and maintenance. In contrast, chamber systems utilize large, open chambers to facilitate water storage and reduced surface footprint. Both systems aim to control runoff, mitigate flooding, and promote groundwater recharge, catering to varying site requirements and environmental regulations.</p></p>
