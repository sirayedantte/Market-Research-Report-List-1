<p><strong>The Car Tyre Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Tyre</li><li>Bias Tyre</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car tyre market is primarily divided into two types: radial tyres and bias tyres. Radial tyres feature a construction where the fabric cords are arranged radially, offering better flexibility, improved fuel efficiency, and enhanced traction, making them more popular for passenger vehicles. In contrast, bias tyres have layers of fabric that run at angles, providing a sturdier sidewall but less flexibility and grip. While bias tyres are often used in older vehicles and specialized applications, radial tyres dominate the modern market due to their superior performance.</p></p>
