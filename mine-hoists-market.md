<p><strong>The Mine Hoists Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Winding Hoists</li><li>Friction Hoists</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mine hoists market encompasses two main types: winding hoists and friction hoists. Winding hoists utilize a drum and cable system to lift materials, providing robust control and reliability for deeper mines. Conversely, friction hoists employ a series of pulleys to grip the cable, offering enhanced efficiency and reduced wear. Both systems are essential for transporting minerals and personnel in mining operations, catering to varying operational requirements and depths, thereby playing a critical role in resource extraction processes.</p></p>
