<p><strong>The Hydro Turbine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Impact Turbine</li><li>Counterattack Turbine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydro turbine market includes various types, primarily impact turbines and counterattack turbines. Impact turbines, such as Pelton wheels, leverage kinetic energy from high-pressure water jets for efficient electricity generation, often used in high-head applications. Counterattack turbines, like Francis turbines, utilize a mix of pressure and kinetic energy by allowing water to flow through them, making them suitable for varying water conditions. Both types play crucial roles in renewable energy production, enhancing the efficiency and sustainability of hydropower systems.</p></p>
