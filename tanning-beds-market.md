<p><ul><li>UVA Tanning Beds</li><li>UVB Tanning Beds</li><li>Combination Tanning Beds</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tanning beds market can be categorized into three main types: UVA tanning beds, UVB tanning beds, and combination tanning beds. UVA tanning beds primarily emit long-wave ultraviolet A rays, which penetrate the skin deeply, promoting a quick tan. UVB tanning beds, on the other hand, emit short-wave ultraviolet B rays, triggering the skin's natural tanning response but can cause more skin damage. Combination tanning beds offer a mix of UVA and UVB rays, catering to diverse tanning preferences and skin types.</p></p>
