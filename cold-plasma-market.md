<p><ul><li>Atmospheric Cold Plasma</li><li>Low-Pressure Cold Plasma</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cold plasma market is divided into two main types: atmospheric cold plasma and low-pressure cold plasma. Atmospheric cold plasma operates at atmospheric pressure, making it suitable for applications in sterilization, wound healing, and surface modification across various industries. In contrast, low-pressure cold plasma requires a vacuum environment, primarily used in semiconductor manufacturing and material processing, providing precise control over surface characteristics. Both types leverage ionized gas to enhance material properties and promote various technological advancements.</p></p>
