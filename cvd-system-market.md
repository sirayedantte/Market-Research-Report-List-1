<p><p>The Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) system market is categorized into Normal CVD and Plasma-Enhanced CVD (PECVD), among others. Normal CVD involves vapor-phase reactions to deposit thin films on substrates, generally at high temperatures, making it suitable for applications like semiconductor manufacturing. PECVD, on the other hand, utilizes plasma to enhance the chemical reaction, allowing for deposition at lower temperatures, which is ideal for delicate materials. Other methods may include innovative techniques like Atomic Layer Deposition (ALD) that cater to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
