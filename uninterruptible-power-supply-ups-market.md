<p><p>The Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) market can be categorized into two main types: DC Power Supply and AC Power Supply. DC Power Supply UPS systems are designed to provide direct current to equipment, suitable for applications requiring constant voltage, such as telecommunications. In contrast, AC Power Supply UPS units convert battery-stored energy into alternating current, ideal for a broader range of electrical devices, including computers and industrial machinery. Both types ensure continuous power during outages, safeguarding sensitive equipment and data.</p></p>
