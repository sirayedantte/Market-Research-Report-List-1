<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Grips</li><li>Mechanical Grips</li><li>Pneumatic Grips</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Materials Test Grips market comprises three main types: hydraulic grips, mechanical grips, and pneumatic grips. </p><p>Hydraulic grips utilize fluid pressure to create a strong clamping force, ideal for high-capacity testing. Mechanical grips rely on mechanical components, such as screws or levers, allowing for manual adjustments, making them versatile for various materials. Pneumatic grips utilize compressed air to achieve rapid and efficient clamping, suited for dynamic testing environments. Each type serves specific testing needs, optimizing efficiency and accuracy in materials testing applications.</p></p>
