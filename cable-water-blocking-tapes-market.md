<p><strong>The Cable Water Blocking Tapes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conductive</li><li>Semi-Conductive</li><li>Non-Conductive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cable water blocking tapes are categorized based on their electrical conductivity properties. Conductive tapes are designed to allow electrical current to pass, often used for grounding and shielding purposes. Semi-conductive tapes combine properties of both conductive and non-conductive types, effectively managing electrical flow while offering moisture protection. Non-conductive tapes, on the other hand, prevent any electrical current, providing insulation and protection against moisture, making them ideal for applications where electrical isolation is essential. Each type serves specific requirements in various cable applications.</p></p>
