<p><p>The corrosion-resistant superalloy market is primarily categorized into three types: iron-based, cobalt-based, and nickel-based superalloys. Iron-based superalloys offer good mechanical properties and oxidation resistance, making them suitable for less extreme applications. Cobalt-based superalloys excel in high-temperature environments and are resistant to wear and corrosion, ideal for aerospace and gas turbine components. Nickel-based superalloys are renowned for their exceptional performance under high-stress, high-temperature conditions, commonly used in aerospace, nuclear, and chemical processing industries due to their superior strength and resistance.</p></p>
