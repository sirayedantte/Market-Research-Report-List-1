<p><p>Pneumatic valve actuators are essential components in automation, utilizing compressed air to control valve operations. The market encompasses three main types: Linear actuators, which provide straight-line motion for gate or globe valves; Right Angle Rotation actuators, designed for quarter-turn valves like ball or butterfly valves, enabling 90-degree movement; and Multirevolution actuators, capable of multiple rotations, typically used for valves requiring extended travel. Each actuator type serves specific control requirements, driving efficiency in various industrial applications.</p></p>
