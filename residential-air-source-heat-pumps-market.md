<p><ul><li>Air-to-Air</li><li>Air-to-Water</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Residential air source heat pumps (ASHPs) are categorized into two main types: air-to-air and air-to-water. Air-to-air heat pumps transfer heat between the outside air and indoor air, effectively providing heating and cooling for homes, making them suitable for moderate climates. Air-to-water heat pumps, on the other hand, extract heat from the air to heat water, which can then be used for central heating systems or domestic hot water, offering versatility for various residential applications.</p></p>
