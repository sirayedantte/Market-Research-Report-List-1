<p><strong>The Stainless Insulated Bottle Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Vacuum Stainless Insulated Bottle</li><li>Vacuum Stainless Insulated Bottle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stainless insulated bottle market is primarily divided into two types: non-vacuum and vacuum stainless insulated bottles. Non-vacuum bottles typically use double-wall construction without vacuum insulation, providing basic thermal retention. In contrast, vacuum stainless insulated bottles feature an airless gap between two walls, significantly enhancing their ability to maintain beverage temperatures for extended periods, whether hot or cold. This distinction affects performance, pricing, and consumer preference, with vacuum bottles generally preferred for superior insulation capabilities.</p></p>
