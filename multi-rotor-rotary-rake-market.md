<p><ul><li>Center Delivery</li><li>Side Delivery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multi-rotor rotary rake market is primarily categorized into center delivery and side delivery rakes. Center delivery rakes collect and centralize hay into uniform windrows, facilitating efficient baling and drying. They are ideal for large fields due to their ability to cover wide areas quickly. In contrast, side delivery rakes spread the material to one or both sides, enhancing air circulation and drying time. Each type serves specific agricultural needs, optimizing the harvesting and handling of crops.</p></p>
