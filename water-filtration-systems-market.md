<p><p>The water filtration systems market comprises various types, including Reverse Osmosis (RO) water filters, Ultrafiltration (UF) membrane water filters, and other filtration methods. RO systems use a semipermeable membrane to remove impurities, providing highly purified water, ideal for households. UF membrane filters utilize size exclusion to separate larger particles and pathogens, maintaining essential minerals. Other market segments may include activated carbon filters, ceramic filters, and UV purification systems, catering to diverse water quality needs and consumer preferences.</p></p>
