<p><p>The On Board Driver Monitoring System (DMS) market is divided into two main types: Active DMS and Passive DMS. Active DMS uses advanced technologies such as cameras and sensors to actively track driver behavior, fatigue, and attention levels in real-time, providing immediate alerts for unsafe conditions. In contrast, Passive DMS collects data on driver behavior without constant monitoring, providing insights through analytics after the fact. Both systems aim to enhance road safety and improve overall driving experiences by addressing driver attentiveness.</p></p>
