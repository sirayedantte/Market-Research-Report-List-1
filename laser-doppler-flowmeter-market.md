<p><p>The Laser Doppler Flowmeter market is segmented into non-invasive perfusion monitoring and invasive microcirculation evaluations. Non-invasive perfusion monitoring utilizes laser technology to measure blood flow through skin or surface tissues without requiring device penetration, making it painless and convenient for patients. In contrast, invasive microcirculation evaluations involve inserting a probe to achieve more precise and accurate blood flow assessments in microcirculatory regions. Both types serve vital roles in clinical diagnostics and research for various medical conditions.</p></p>
