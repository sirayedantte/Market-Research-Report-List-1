<p><p>The MRI system market is categorized into closed and open MRI systems. Closed MRI systems feature a cylindrical design, offering high-resolution images and greater comfort for patients with less anxiety. They are suitable for a wide range of clinical applications. Open MRI systems, on the other hand, provide a more open and less claustrophobic environment, making it ideal for patients with anxiety or those who are larger in size. However, they may have lower image quality compared to closed systems.</p></p>
