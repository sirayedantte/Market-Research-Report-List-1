<p><p>The tidal stream generators market consists of several types, each designed to harness tidal energy efficiently. Axial turbines operate like wind turbines, extracting energy by rotating around a horizontal axis. Crossflow turbines capture energy from water flowing in a horizontal manner, enhancing efficiency in low-flow conditions. Flow augmented turbines utilize aerodynamic features to increase water flow speed over the blades. Other market types may include innovative designs and hybrid systems that combine various technologies for optimized energy generation.</p></p>
