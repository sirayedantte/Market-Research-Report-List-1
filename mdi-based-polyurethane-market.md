<p><strong>The MDI Based Polyurethane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pure MDI</li><li>Polymeric MDI (PMDI)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MDI-based polyurethane market comprises two primary types: pure MDI and polymeric MDI (PMDI). Pure MDI is a monomer used primarily in rigid foam applications, offering high-performance characteristics and chemical resistance. In contrast, PMDI is a mixture of MDI oligomers, commonly employed in flexible foams, coatings, and adhesives due to its enhanced processing characteristics. Both types are integral to the production of various polyurethane products, serving diverse industrial applications, including construction, automotive, and furniture industries.</p></p>
