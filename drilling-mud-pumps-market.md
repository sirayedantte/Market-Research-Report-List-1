<p><strong>The Drilling Mud Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-acting Pumps</li><li>Double-acting Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drilling mud pumps market comprises primarily two types: single-acting and double-acting pumps. Single-acting pumps utilize one piston to send fluid in a single direction per cycle, making them simpler but less efficient for high-volume needs. In contrast, double-acting pumps feature pistons that operate on both strokes, allowing for continuous fluid flow and greater efficiency, especially in demanding applications. Both types are critical for maintaining pressure and transporting drilling fluids in various drilling operations.</p></p>
