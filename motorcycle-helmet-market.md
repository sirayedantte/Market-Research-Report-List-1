<p><p>The motorcycle helmet market comprises several types, each designed for specific riding preferences and safety needs. Full face helmets offer maximum protection with a complete enclosure, ideal for high-speed rides. Open face helmets provide coverage for the head and ears while allowing more air circulation, suited for cruising. Half helmets, or brain buckets, offer minimal coverage primarily for urban riding. Off-road helmets are designed for motocross and dirt biking, featuring increased ventilation and a distinctive visor to protect against debris.</p></p>
