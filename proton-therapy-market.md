<p><p>The proton therapy market encompasses various types of particle therapies used in cancer treatment. Electron beam therapy primarily treats surface tumors, while proton beam therapy targets tumors with precision, minimizing damage to surrounding tissues. Neutron beam therapy utilizes high-energy neutrons for specific tumors, and carbon ion beam therapy boasts enhanced therapeutic potential due to its heavier particles. Alpha particle beam therapy targets cancerous cells with high energy, whereas beta particle beam therapy focuses on treating solid tumors with lower energy levels, offering diverse treatment options.</p></p>
