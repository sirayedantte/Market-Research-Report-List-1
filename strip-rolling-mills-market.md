<p><strong>The Strip Rolling Mills Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two-High Rolling Mills</li><li>Four-High Rolling Mills</li><li>Six-High Rolling Mills</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The strip rolling mills market encompasses various configurations, each designed for specific production needs. Two-high rolling mills have two horizontal rolls and are ideal for forming thicker materials. Four-high rolling mills feature two smaller rolls in the middle between two larger rolls, allowing for better pressure control and efficiency in processing thinner strips. Six-high rolling mills add two additional smaller rolls for enhanced flexibility and precision in cold rolling applications, resulting in improved product quality and surface finish.</p></p>
