<p><p>The coated backsheet market is segmented into two primary types: fluoropolymer and non-fluoropolymer. Fluoropolymer backsheets are known for their exceptional chemical resistance and weatherability, making them ideal for high-performance applications in solar panels and electrical devices. In contrast, non-fluoropolymer backsheets, which may include materials like polyethylene or other polymers, are cost-effective alternatives offering adequate protection against environmental factors. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing the durability and functionality of various electronic and photovoltaic products.</p></p>
