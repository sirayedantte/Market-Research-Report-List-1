<p><strong>The Cyclodextrin Derivatives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HP-β-CD</li><li>SBE-β-CD</li><li>RM-β-CD</li><li>HP-γ-CD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cyclodextrin derivatives are specialized carbohydrates with various market types due to their unique properties. HP-β-CD (hydroxypropyl-β-cyclodextrin) is known for enhancing solubility and stability of pharmaceuticals. SBE-β-CD (sulfobutylether-β-cyclodextrin) offers high solubility and is often used in parenteral formulations. RM-β-CD (randomly methylated-β-cyclodextrin) is utilized for its ability to improve drug delivery and reduce toxicity. HP-γ-CD (hydroxypropyl-γ-cyclodextrin) has a larger cavity, making it suitable for encapsulating larger molecules and improving bioavailability in various applications.</p></p>
