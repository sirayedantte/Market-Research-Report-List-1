<p><ul><li>Two-terminal HVDC System</li><li>Multi-terminal HVDC System (MTDC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HVDC system market primarily comprises two types: the two-terminal HVDC system and the multi-terminal HVDC system (MTDC). Two-terminal HVDC systems consist of two stations connected by a direct current line, optimizing long-distance power transmission. In contrast, MTDC systems involve multiple interconnected stations, enhancing grid flexibility and integration of renewable energy sources. Both systems are pivotal in modernizing power grids, reducing transmission losses, and enabling efficient energy distribution across vast distances, supporting global energy transition efforts.</p></p>
