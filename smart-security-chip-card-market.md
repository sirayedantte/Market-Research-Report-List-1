<p><p>The Smart Security Chip Card Market is divided into two main types: contact and contactless cards. Contact cards require physical insertion into a card reader, allowing for secure data transmission through direct connection. In contrast, contactless cards utilize radio-frequency identification (RFID) technology, enabling transactions with just a tap or wave near a compatible reader. Both types enhance security and streamline payment processes, catering to different consumer preferences and use cases in various sectors, including banking, retail, and access control.</p></p>
