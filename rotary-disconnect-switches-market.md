<p><ul><li>Fused Rotary Disconnect Switches</li><li>Non-Fused Rotary Disconnect Switches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotary disconnect switches market is primarily divided into two types: fused and non-fused rotary disconnect switches. Fused rotary disconnect switches incorporate fuses within the design for overcurrent protection, ensuring safe disconnection and protecting connected equipment from faults. In contrast, non-fused rotary disconnect switches do not include fuses, relying on external protection devices for safety. Each type serves distinct applications, with fused switches often used in scenarios requiring built-in protection, while non-fused options offer simplicity and cost-effectiveness in various industrial settings.</p></p>
