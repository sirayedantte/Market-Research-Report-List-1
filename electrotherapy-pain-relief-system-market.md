<p><p>The Electrotherapy Pain Relief System market includes various modalities, primarily Transcutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulation (TENS) and Microcurrent Nerve Stimulation (MENS). TENS delivers low-voltage electrical currents through the skin to alleviate pain by stimulating nerves and blocking pain signals. In contrast, MENS uses very low electrical currents to promote tissue healing and reduce pain at a cellular level. Both modalities are non-invasive, widely used for pain management, and appeal to consumers seeking alternative therapy options.</p></p>
