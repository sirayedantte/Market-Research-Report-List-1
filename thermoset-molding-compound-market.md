Epoxy resins offer superior adhesion and mechanical properties, while polyester resins are versatile for various applications. Urea formaldehyde resins are used in adhesives and coatings, and melamine formaldehyde resins provide durability and a glossy finish. Other compounds include hybrid or specialty resins catering to niche applications.</p></p>
