Fin antennas are sleek and often mounted on vehicle rooftops, providing a low-profile design. Rod antennas are traditional and long, mainly used for radio signals. Integrated antennas combine multiple functions into a single unit, optimizing space and performance, while "other" encompasses various niche designs.</p></p>
