Each variant offers unique properties suited for diverse applications, with PHB being the most common, while PHBV enhances flexibility and processability. PHBHx provides additional elasticity, and PHB4B allows for improved degradation rates. Other PHA types may include blends and copolymers, catering to specific needs in biodegradable packaging and other sustainable products.</p></p>
