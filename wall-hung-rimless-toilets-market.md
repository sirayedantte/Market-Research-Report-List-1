<p><p>The wall hung rimless toilet market can be categorized into two main types: monolithic and separate. Monolithic toilets feature a single, seamless design that combines the bowl and mounting system into one unit, providing a sleek, modern aesthetic and easier cleaning. In contrast, separate wall hung toilets consist of a bowl mounted to the wall with an independent cistern behind it, allowing for more flexibility in design and installation. Both types offer space-saving solutions and enhanced hygiene through their rimless design.</p></p>
