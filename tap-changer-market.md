<p><strong>The Tap Changer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>OLTC</li><li>DETC/NLTC/OCTC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tap changer market encompasses various types of devices used to regulate voltage in transformers. The On-Load Tap Changer (OLTC) adjusts voltage dynamically while the transformer is under load, ensuring stable output. The De-Energized Tap Changer (DETC) or No-Load Tap Changer (NLTC) requires the transformer to be de-energized for adjustments, making it less flexible. The Off-Circuit Tap Changer (OCTC) operates similarly to DETC but allows for adjustments in a circuit. These variances cater to different operational and efficiency needs in power systems.</p></p>
