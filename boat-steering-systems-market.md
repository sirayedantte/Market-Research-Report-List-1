<p><p>The Boat Steering Systems market encompasses various types, including manual steering, hydraulic steering, electric power steering, and electro-hydraulic steering. Manual steering relies on mechanical components for direct control, while hydraulic steering utilizes fluid pressure for smoother handling. Electric power steering provides enhanced responsiveness using electric actuators, improving maneuverability. Electro-hydraulic steering combines hydraulic systems with electric assistance, offering precise control and reduced effort. Each system caters to different boat types and user preferences, affecting performance and comfort on the water.</p></p>
