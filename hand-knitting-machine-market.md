<p><strong>The Hand Knitting Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Circular Machine</li><li>Flat Knitting Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hand knitting machine market primarily consists of two types: circular machines and flat knitting machines. Circular machines produce tubular garments, utilizing a continuous loop of yarn, ideal for items like socks and hats. In contrast, flat knitting machines create flat fabric pieces that are later sewn together, suitable for garments like sweaters and cardigans. Both types cater to different knitting needs, enabling various patterns and textures, making them essential tools for hobbyists and professionals in textile production.</p></p>
