<p><p>The Loose Fill Polystyrene Packaging Market encompasses various types, each with unique properties. Ordinary Polystyrene is basic and used for low-cost applications. Expanded Polystyrene (EPS) is lightweight, offers excellent cushioning, and is moisture-resistant, making it ideal for shipping fragile items. High-Impact Polystyrene (HIPS) combines strength and toughness, suitable for more demanding applications. Solid Polystyrene (SPS) is used where structural integrity is crucial. Together, these materials provide versatile solutions for protecting products during transportation and storage.</p></p>
