<p><ul><li>Underwater Float Types</li><li>Caisson Types</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semi-submersible drilling platforms are categorized primarily into two market types: underwater float types and caisson types. Underwater float types feature buoyant structures that remain partially submerged to provide stability in rough seas, allowing for efficient drilling operations. Conversely, caisson types rely on large cylindrical structures that are anchored to the seabed, providing additional weight and stability. Both designs serve to optimize drilling efficiency and safety in various marine environments, playing a crucial role in offshore oil and gas exploration.</p></p>
